## Visual and UX Checks

1. No horizontal scroll.
2. Header/nav remains usable.
3. Mobile menu opens/closes correctly.
4. Language switcher is accessible and functional.
5. Buttons are readable and tappable.
6. Forms fit viewport without clipped fields.
7. CTA cards do not overlap content.
8. AdSlot placeholders (if shown) do not break layout.
9. Footer spacing and links remain clean.

## Localization Display Checks

1. Assamese pages wrap text cleanly without clipping.
2. Hindi pages wrap text cleanly without clipping.
3. No mojibake/encoding corruption in localized headings and body text.
4. No visible translation key placeholders in UI.
