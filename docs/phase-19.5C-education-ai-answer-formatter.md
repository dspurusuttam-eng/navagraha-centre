## Student safety behavior
- No guaranteed marks, rank, admission, scholarship, pass/fail, or exam success claims.
- No fear-based exam language.
- No deterministic failure language.
- No remedy guarantee language.
- Severe exam stress, hopelessness, self-harm, panic, or emotional danger falls back to the existing crisis-support behavior.

## Missing-chart behavior
- If chart context is missing, the formatter keeps the answer general.
- The user is guided to generate or refresh Kundli context before expecting deeper study-specific timing.
- The formatter does not fabricate missing academic certainty.

## Premium behavior
- Free/premium limits were not changed.
- No premium bypass was introduced.
- Deeper education/career report or consultation suggestions remain soft and contextual.
