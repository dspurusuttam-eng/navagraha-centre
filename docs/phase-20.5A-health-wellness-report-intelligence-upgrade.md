## Missing-Context Behavior
- If chart context is unavailable, the report falls back to safe summary language instead of fabricating wellness certainty.
- D12 or other refinement charts are only referenced when actually present in saved chart context.
- Missing Dasha, transit, or yoga context is surfaced as unavailable rather than predicted.

## Medical Safety Wording Rules
- No diagnosis.
- No medicine advice.
- No stopping medicine advice.
- No cure claim.
- No prediction of death, serious illness, or hospitalization.
- No fear-based health wording.
- No remedy cure guarantee.
- The report is astrology-informed wellness guidance only.
- Symptoms or urgent concerns should be directed to qualified healthcare help.
