# Phase 19.8C - Remedies / Spiritual Guidance AI Answer Formatter

## Files Changed
- `src/modules/ask-chart/jyotish-answer-formatter.ts`

## Remedy Answer Structure
- `Remedy Summary`
- `Why This Remedy Is Suggested`
- `Simple Practice Steps`
- `Best Timing / Discipline`
- `Caution / Safety Note`
- `Optional Product or Consultation Guidance`
- `Soft Next Step`
- `Confidence` and the existing astrology disclaimer remain appended as in other formatter branches

## When the Formatter Applies
- Applies to explicit remedy and spiritual guidance questions, including:
  - remedies
  - mantra or prayer
  - puja
  - charity or daan
  - fasting or discipline
  - graha remedy
  - daily remedy
  - gemstone, rudraksha, or yantra guidance
  - spiritual support
- Remedy handling takes precedence over the daily-prediction branch when the user is clearly asking for a remedy-oriented answer.

## Safety Behavior
- Remedies stay optional and non-fear-based.
- No guaranteed result language is introduced.
- No “must do or bad result will happen” wording is used.
- No medical, financial, or legal cure claims are allowed.
- Gemstone, rudraksha, and yantra guidance remains consultative and optional.
- The formatter keeps remedy guidance grounded in approved remedy records when present.

## Shop / Consultation Ethics
- Shop references are soft and only appear when product guidance is relevant.
- Consultation references stay optional and are used for personalized or serious cases.
- No purchase pressure is introduced.
- The formatter does not frame any product as required.

## Missing-Chart Behavior
- If chart context is missing, the reply stays general.
- The answer directs the user to generate or refresh Kundli context for a more personalized remedy reading.
- No fabricated remedy certainty is introduced.
