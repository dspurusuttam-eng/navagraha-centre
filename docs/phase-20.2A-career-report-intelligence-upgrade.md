## Missing-Context Behavior
- When chart data is unavailable, the report falls back to safe summary language instead of fabricating chart-specific certainty.
- D10 / Dashamsa is only mentioned when it is actually present in the saved chart context.
- Missing dasha or transit data is surfaced as an unavailable timing context, not as a prediction.

## Safety Wording Rules
- No guaranteed job, promotion, role-change, or income claims.
- No fear-based work-pressure language.
- No medical, legal, financial, or investment certainty.
- No coercive relationship wording.
- Optional remedies only, with no guarantee language.
- No raw chart or internal context exposure.
