## Missing-Context Behavior
- If chart context is unavailable, the report falls back to safe summary language instead of fabricating financial certainty.
- D10 / Dashamsa is only referenced when it is actually present in the saved chart context.
- Missing timing, transit, or wealth-rule data is surfaced as unavailable rather than predicted.

## Financial Safety Wording Rules
- No guaranteed income, profit, wealth, or sudden gain claims.
- No exact money amount prediction.
- No investment advice.
- No stock, crypto, gambling, betting, or lottery recommendations.
- No legal, tax, or financial certainty.
- No fear-based language around loss or debt.
- No remedy guarantee claims.
- Optional remedies only and clearly non-deterministic.
