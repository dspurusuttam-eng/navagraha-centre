## Final Business Answer Structure
- Business Summary
- Business / Job / Partnership Tendency
- Timing Insight
- Growth Opportunities
- Risk / Caution Areas
- Practical Business Guidance
- Optional Spiritual Support
- Soft Next Step
- Caution / Safety Note when relevant

## When Business Formatter Applies
- Business suitability
- Job vs business
- Startup
- Side business
- Partnership
- Trade / commerce
- Client / customer growth
- Business expansion
- Foreign business
- Family business
- Business profit timing
- Business debt / loan pressure
- Business investment / risk
- Business remedies

## Business / Finance / Legal Safety Behavior
- No guaranteed profit, success, clients, funding, expansion, or debt removal claims.
- No investment advice or product recommendations.
- No stock, crypto, gambling, betting, or lottery guidance.
- No legal or tax certainty claims.
- No reckless risk-taking or fear-based debt language.
- Remedies remain optional and supportive only.

## Missing-Chart Behavior
- If chart context is missing, the answer stays general.
- The formatter tells the user that deeper business guidance needs birth details or a generated Kundli.
- It does not fabricate chart certainty.

## Premium Behavior
- Free and premium limits remain unchanged.
- Business/Career/Finance report or consultation mentions stay soft and contextual.
- No premium bypass or pressure was introduced.
